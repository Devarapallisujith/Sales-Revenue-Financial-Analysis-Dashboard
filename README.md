# Sales-Revenue-Financial-Analysis-Dashboard
## Description
This project presents an interactive Sales & Revenue Financial Analysis Dashboard developed using Power BI. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of business performance by analyzing total revenue, average revenue, regional contribution, store performance, product rankings, and sales representative activity.
It enables users to monitor financial metrics, identify high-performing regions and stores, and track revenue trends over time.
## Purpose
The purpose of this project is to:
-	Track overall financial performance
-	Analyze revenue distribution across regions
-	Compare store performance
-	Identify top products by revenue
-	Evaluate sales representative performance
-	Monitor quarterly and monthly revenue trends
## Tech Stack
-	Power BI
-	Power Query (ETL & Data Transformation)
-	DAX (Measures & Calculations)
-	Data Modeling
## Data Source
Sample sales and financial dataset containing:
-	Date (Year, Quarter, Month)
-	Region
-	Country
-	Store
-	Products
-	Sales Representatives
-	Revenue
-	Transactions

## Business Questions Solved by the Dashboard
-	What is the total revenue, average revenue, and total transactions?
-	Which regions contribute the most revenue?
-	How does revenue vary by country?
-	Which stores are top performers in terms of revenue?
-	What are the top products based on revenue and ranking?
-	How does revenue change across quarters and months?
-	Which sales representatives generate the highest revenue?
-	How does average revenue compare with total revenue trends?
-	Are there any seasonal or quarterly sales patterns?
-	Which areas show growth opportunities or lower performance?
## Example Scenario
-	A business manager uses this dashboard to identify which regions and stores generate the highest revenue, evaluate sales team performance, and track revenue trends across quarters to support strategic financial planning
## Conclusion
-	The Sales & Revenue Financial Analysis Dashboard provides a clear overview of business financial performance by combining revenue, regional insights, store analysis, and sales team evaluation in one place.
-	The dashboard reveals that revenue is concentrated in specific regions, stores, and products, while performance varies across time periods and sales representatives. By identifying top performers and underperforming areas, the dashboard helps stakeholders make informed decisions, optimize sales strategies, and focus on growth opportunities.
